Features

Features

Multiple AR and VR devices

- Pengram can connect multiple AR and VR devices together, so that you can have multiple intructors and trainees participate in one session.

Corporate account authentication & Coporate lobby

- Login with your corporate account to go into the private lobby exclusively and securely designed for your company.

Multiple “classrooms”

- After login, the user is presented with a list of available classrooms upon login. Multiple classrooms allow you to support multiple operations at the same time.

Model Storage, Upload and Download

- Now you can choose to safely upload your models to our cloud and dynamically download it into your classroom.

Classroom Features (General)

Telepresence

- You can now see your colleague’s hands and head live almost as if they were next to you.

Audio Conference

- You can hear and talk to everybody in the classroom.

Classroom Features (AR, Trainee specific)

Aligning virtual model to real world object

While in classroom, you can load up a virtual object, and align it with the real object you are receiving intructions for.
- Manually using Vuforia. While in a classroom, simply hold on a piece of marker corresponding to the model, and look at it. After the model appears, you can move it around by moving your marker.
- Auto aligning (coming soon)

Ghost model

While you are looking at the annotations on the real object, we also provide a ghost model on the side for you to reference. Don’t worry, all of the annotations are synchronized over.

Classroom Features (VR, Instructor specific)

Locomotion

Moving around the virtual space simply using your controllers’ joystick. Using your right joystick to move in the direction you are looking at, or can move left joystick left and right to adjust your view accordingly. [Note: On Vive, touch on the touchpad instead of moving the joystick.]

Scaling

Scale yourself for more precise annotations and instructions.
- To zoom in, hold on the “Grab” key on both controllers, then move your hands apart - To zoom out, hold on the “Grab” key on both controllers, then move your hands together

Video stream

Seeing how and what your trainee is doing and verifying the correctness while staying in the virtual world. The video stream will appear on a tablet in VR classrooms.

Menu

Instructors can access a list of features and tools by simply using the menu on their left hand.

- Annotations

Illustrate and point using lines and arrows. Point the line or arrow options on the menu using your right hand, then select using the right “Trigger” button, you should see a shooting gun appearing on your right wrist. Now hold on the “Trigger” key to starting drawing line or arrow, when you are done, release the “Trigger” key.

- Erase

Not satisfied with your drawings? You can erase them. Point to the Eraser option on the menu using your right hand, the press “Trigger” to select. An eraser should appear on your right wrist. Touch the line or arrow that you want to erase using the eraser and press “Trigger” to erase.

Instructors can access a list of features and tools by simply using the menu on their left hand.

Grab

You can interact with virtual objects by pressing down the “Grab” button. The item you can grab will be highlighted.

Snap

Putting the part close to where it should be, it will snap back to where they were like magnets.

Reset

Your view gets too messy? You can reset it all using the “Reset” button on your left hand menu.

Common tools

We provide a set of common mechanical tools to facilitate your instructions. To select a tool, move the left joystick up to see “Advanced menu”. From here, you have two tabs “Tools” and “Download”. Select “Tools” tab, by pointing your right hand and press “Trigger”. Then you can browse and select from a list of available tools. Once selected, the tool will appear near you, and you can start to interact with it.
